ABOUT



J.S.C stands for Jenny, Shannon, and Cindy. In the summer of 2017, these three girls received an instruction that they were going to create a website for a desert shop; thus, they started contacting the process of making a desert for the first time in their lives.

Not so long after starting constructing the website, Jenny, Shannon, and Cindy found that they were actually interested in finding deserts and inventing new types of deserts. Finally, they decided to quit programming and literally opened a shop named J.S.C
